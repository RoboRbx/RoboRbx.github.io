Robo About Me

What is Robo?

Robo is an up and coming utility for ROBLOX group owners. Robo will be based around group & game Management.

Why choose Robo?

Robo is:

  • lightweight
  • reliable
  • maintained
  • feature-rich
  • easy to use
  • unique

When can I use it?

Robo is still in development, but rest assured that we are actively working on it.

What will I be able to do with Robo?

With Robo, you will be able to easily manage group ranks, shouts, etc. and you will be able to manage game moderation, retention, etc. without opening ROBLOX, ROBLOX Studio or the ROBLOX creator dashboard.
